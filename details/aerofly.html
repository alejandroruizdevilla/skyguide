Overview

Aerofly FS 4 is the simulator that prioritizes one thing above all else: performance. If you want a flight simulator that runs at 60+ FPS even in VR on mid-range hardware, Aerofly is your answer. Developed by IPACS, it delivers an incredibly smooth, responsive experience that makes flying feel effortless.

While it may not have the global coverage of MSFS or the physics depth of X-Plane, Aerofly excels at providing a beautiful, enjoyable flying experience with instant loading times, butter-smooth framerates, and some of the best VR implementation in any simulator. It's perfect for casual flight sessions and helicopter enthusiasts.

Perfect for: VR enthusiasts wanting smooth performance, helicopter simulation fans, casual pilots who value quick sessions, and anyone with mid-range hardware who wants beautiful graphics without compromising framerates.

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Exceptional performance - runs smoothly on almost anything
  • Best VR experience of any simulator (performance-wise)
  • Instant loading - be flying in seconds
  • Beautiful photogrammetry in covered areas
  • Excellent helicopter models and physics
  • No stutters or hitching
  • Great for quick casual sessions
  • Mobile version is outstanding
  • Perfect for VR helicopter flying
  • Easy to pick up and fly

Limitations

  • Limited geographic coverage (mainly US West, Switzerland)
  • Smaller aircraft selection
  • DLC can be expensive for region coverage
  • No global scenery like MSFS
  • Limited weather system
  • Systems depth not as advanced as X-Plane/PMDG
  • Smaller community and add-on ecosystem
  • No multiplayer currently

Getting Started

For New Pilots

Aerofly is very beginner-friendly. Start with the Cessna 172 or Baron 58. The quick loading means you can practice takeoffs and landings repeatedly without frustration. Use a controller or joystick for best experience.

Recommended First Flight

  • Aircraft: Cessna 172 or Baron 58
  • Location: KSFO (San Francisco) for beautiful scenery
  • Weather: Clear day
  • Activity: Sightseeing over the Bay Area
  • Time: 15-30 minute flight

For VR Users

  • Try the EC135 helicopter in VR
  • Fly through San Francisco's photogrammetry
  • Experience butter-smooth 90+ FPS
  • Use hand controllers for switches (if supported)
  • Enjoy no motion sickness due to high framerates

Recommended DLC

  • Switzerland region (stunning Alps scenery)
  • Additional US regions if you want more coverage
  • Helicopter bundles if you like rotorcraft
  • Wait for sales - DLC goes on discount regularly

Best Scenarios

  • VR flight sessions - unmatched smoothness
  • Helicopter training and practice
  • Quick casual flights
  • Sightseeing in photogrammetry areas
  • Mobile flying on tablets

Who Should Choose Aerofly FS 4?
