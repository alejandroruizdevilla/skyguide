Overview

RFS - Real Flight Simulator brings realistic aircraft systems and flight procedures to mobile devices. More accessible than Infinite Flight but more realistic than arcade games, RFS strikes a good balance for mobile pilots who want authentic procedures without overwhelming complexity.

With detailed aircraft, weather simulation, and proper flight planning tools, RFS is perfect for casual pilots who want to experience realistic flying sessions on their phone or tablet.

Perfect for: Mobile pilots wanting realistic but accessible simulation, beginners learning procedures, casual flying on phones/tablets, and those who find Infinite Flight too serious.

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Free to download and try
  • Good balance of realism and accessibility
  • Realistic aircraft systems
  • Weather simulation

Limitations

  • Aircraft must be purchased individually
  • Smaller community than Infinite Flight
  • No live ATC
  • Limited scenery detail
  • Can get expensive with many aircraft

Getting Started
