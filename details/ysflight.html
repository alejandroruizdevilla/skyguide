Overview

YSFlight is a charming, lightweight flight simulator that proves you don't need cutting-edge graphics to have fun flying. Developed by one person (Soji Yamakawa) since 1999, it runs on absolutely anything - from modern PCs to ancient laptops - making it accessible to everyone.

With simplified controls, instant action, and a huge library of community-created aircraft (1000+), YSFlight is perfect for casual flying fun, multiplayer dogfights, and introducing kids to flight simulation. It's the ultimate "pick up and play" flight sim.

Perfect for: Kids and beginners, retro gaming enthusiasts, anyone with low-end hardware, casual multiplayer dogfights, and people who want simple flying fun without complexity.
