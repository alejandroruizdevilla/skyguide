Overview

Falcon BMS (Benchmark Sims) is a community-driven modification of the legendary Falcon 4.0 that has evolved into the most realistic F-16C Viper simulator available to civilians. This is not just a game - it's a professional-grade training tool that models the F-16 with incredible depth and accuracy, completely free.

What makes BMS special is its legendary dynamic campaign engine that generates living, breathing wars where your actions have real consequences. Combined with modern avionics (Link-16, JHMCS, advanced weapons), it offers the most complete F-16 experience possible.

Perfect for: F-16 enthusiasts, serious simulation fans on a budget, anyone who wants the most detailed single aircraft study-level sim, and players who love dynamic campaigns.

Pros & Cons

Limitations

Getting Started

Installation

  • Purchase Falcon 4.0 on GOG or Steam (~$5-10)
  • Download latest Falcon BMS from benchmark sims website
  • Install BMS over Falcon 4.0
  • Apply alternative launchers for easier setup

For New Pilots

Expect 50-100 hours to become proficient. Start with training missions. Learn basic startup, navigation, and landing before weapons. Use the comprehensive manual and online tutorials.

Essential Learning

  • IFF setup (critical for avoiding fratricide)
  • Basic radar operation
  • Datalink usage (Link-16)
  • Weapon employment procedures
  • Emergency procedures

Hardware Needed

  • HOTAS strongly recommended (Thrustmaster Warthog ideal)
  • Head tracking essential (TrackIR or alternative)
  • Rudder pedals recommended
  • MFD couplers enhance immersion
