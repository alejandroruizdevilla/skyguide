Overview

Ace Combat 7: Skies Unknown is pure arcade flight action wrapped in Hollywood-style presentation. This isn't a simulator - it's a cinematic experience where you become an ace pilot in an alternate reality, engaging in spectacular dogfights while an epic story unfolds around you.

With incredible soundtracks, dramatic storylines, and accessible controls that let anyone feel like a fighter ace, Ace Combat 7 is perfect for those who want exciting aerial combat without the complexity of study-level sims.

Perfect for: Players wanting arcade action over realism, fans of dramatic storytelling, those intimidated by complex sims, gamers who love spectacle, and anyone wanting pick-up-and-play aerial combat fun.

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Incredibly fun and accessible
  • Amazing soundtrack and presentation
  • Easy to pick up and play
  • Spectacular combat
  • Great for casual sessions
  • VR missions are incredible (PSVR)
  • Engaging campaign story
  • Controller-friendly

Limitations

  • Not realistic simulation
  • Campaign relatively short
  • Multiplayer community small
  • VR only on PSVR (not PC)
  • Can feel repetitive
  • Story can be confusing

Getting Started

  • Available on Steam, PlayStation, Xbox
  • Use controller (better than KB+M)
  • Play campaign for full experience
  • Start with easier difficulty
  • Unlock aircraft through progression
  • Perfect for couch gaming sessions
