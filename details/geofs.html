Overview

GeoFS is a free, browser-based flight simulator that runs entirely in your web browser using WebGL technology. It uses real satellite imagery and elevation data to create a global flying environment without requiring any downloads or installation.

What makes GeoFS special is its accessibility - you can be flying within seconds just by visiting the website. It's perfect for quick flights, introducing friends to flight simulation, or flying when you don't have access to your main PC.

Perfect for: Beginners, casual flyers, those without gaming PCs, quick sessions, introducing others to flight simulation, and flying from any device with a browser.

Why Choose GeoFS?

Zero Barrier to Entry: No downloads, no expensive hardware, no complicated setup. Just open a browser and fly.

Cross-Platform: Start a flight on your PC, continue on your tablet. GeoFS works everywhere.

Active Community: Regular updates, active Discord server, and community-created content keep the experience fresh.

Educational Value: Great for learning basic flight principles before investing in more complex simulators.

Surprisingly Capable: Despite being browser-based, GeoFS offers a respectable flight model and feature set.

Limitations

While GeoFS is impressive for a browser sim, it has some limitations compared to desktop simulators:

  • Simplified flight models compared to study-level sims
  • Lower graphical fidelity than MSFS or X-Plane
  • Limited airport detail
  • Basic cockpit systems
  • Dependent on internet connection
Best Used As: A complement to desktop simulators, a way to introduce new pilots to aviation, or for casual flying when you can't access your main setup.

Getting Started

  • Visit geo-fs.com in any modern browser
  • Click "Start Flying" - no account needed
  • Use keyboard/mouse or connect a joystick
  • Choose an aircraft and airport
  • Start exploring the world!
