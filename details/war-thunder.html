Overview

War Thunder is a massive free-to-play combat game spanning WW1 biplanes to modern jets, with 2000+ vehicles across aircraft, tanks, and naval vessels. It offers three difficulty modes: Arcade (casual), Realistic (balanced), and Simulator (full sim controls), making it accessible to everyone from casual gamers to serious sim enthusiasts.

With 800+ aircraft alone, War Thunder lets you experience aviation history from early prop fighters to supersonic jets, all in a persistent progression system with regular updates and events.

Perfect for: Free-to-play fans, casual to intermediate combat enthusiasts, players who want variety across eras, those interested in historical vehicles, and gamers wanting combined arms battles.

Key Features

Massive Vehicle Selection

  • 800+ aircraft from WW1 to modern
  • 1200+ tanks and ground vehicles
  • 200+ naval vessels
  • All major nations represented
  • Regular additions and updates

Three Difficulty Modes

  • Arcade: Simplified, fast-paced action
  • Realistic: Balanced sim/game hybrid
  • Simulator: Full simulation controls
  • Choose your preferred difficulty
  • Each mode has separate matchmaking

Combined Arms

  • Play aircraft, tanks, or ships
  • Combined battles with all vehicle types
  • Switch between vehicle classes
  • Air support for ground forces
  • Dynamic gameplay

Progression

  • Research tech trees by nation
  • Unlock vehicles through gameplay
  • Customize vehicles and loadouts
  • Historical and fictional camos
  • Crew skills and upgrades

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Completely free to play
  • 2000+ vehicles total
  • Three difficulty modes for all skill levels
  • Beautiful graphics
  • Cross-platform play
  • Regular updates and events
  • Huge player base

Limitations

  • Progression can be slow (F2P grind)
  • Premium vehicles provide advantages
  • Can feel pay-to-progress
  • Balance issues at times
  • Simulator mode less populated
  • Economy changes controversy
