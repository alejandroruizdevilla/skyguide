Overview

IL-2 Sturmovik: Great Battles series is the definitive World War 2 combat flight simulation. With meticulously researched aircraft from all major nations, realistic damage modeling, and immersive career modes, it brings the air war of 1939-1945 to life like no other sim.

Unlike arcade WW2 games, IL-2 delivers authentic flight models where each aircraft has its own characteristics, strengths, and weaknesses. You'll learn to manage engine temperatures, fuel mixture, and ammunition conservation while engaging in intense dogfights over historically accurate battlefields.

Perfect for: WW2 aviation enthusiasts, history buffs, VR combat flight fans, and anyone who wants authentic propeller-driven combat with gorgeous graphics and excellent performance.

Getting Started

Which Module to Buy First?

  • Battle of Stalingrad - Eastern Front 1942-43, great variety
  • Battle of Bodenplatte - Western Front 1944-45, P-51s and late war
  • Battle of Kuban - Eastern Front 1943, excellent aircraft selection

For Beginners

Start with easier aircraft like the Yak-1 or early Bf 109. Use simplified engine management initially. Practice takeoffs and landings before combat. The career mode is an excellent teacher.

Essential Settings

  • Enable simplified engine controls initially
  • Use head tracking or VR for situational awareness
  • Start with normal difficulty
  • Practice offline before multiplayer
