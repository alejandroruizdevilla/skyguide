Overview

The latest iteration of Microsoft's legendary flight simulator series, MSFS 2024 sets the gold standard for visual fidelity and real-world accuracy in flight simulation. Built on cutting-edge technology, it features the entire planet rendered with photorealistic satellite imagery, real-time weather systems, and a truly living world with AI-generated traffic.

This is not just a simulator‚Äîit's a complete aviation experience that lets you explore every corner of the Earth from the cockpit of highly detailed aircraft. Whether you want to fly over your hometown, experience the Northern Lights from a Cessna 172, or pilot an Airbus A320 across continents, MSFS 2024 delivers an unmatched level of immersion.

Perfect for: Sightseeing flights, VFR flying, casual to serious simulation, and anyone who values stunning graphics and realistic scenery. Ideal for experiencing flight over your hometown or traveling the world virtually.

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Unmatched visual quality and photorealistic scenery
  • Entire world to explore with real-time weather
  • Excellent for VFR (visual flight) operations
  • Strong add-on ecosystem with quality third-party aircraft
  • Career mode adds structure for new pilots
  • Available on Game Pass (great value)
  • Regular updates and improvements
  • Accessible to beginners with flight assists

Limitations

  • Requires high-end hardware for best experience
  • Large download size and storage requirements
  • Internet connection required for full features
  • Some default aircraft lack systems depth
  • Performance can be inconsistent in dense areas
  • Learning curve for complex aircraft

Getting Started

For Beginners

Start with the Flight Training modules included in the sim. Begin with the Cessna 152 or 172 with all flight assists enabled. Practice takeoffs and landings at your local airport. Gradually disable assists as you gain confidence.

Recommended First Flight

  • Aircraft: Cessna 172 (G1000)
  • Location: Your nearest airport
  • Weather: Clear skies, light winds
  • Time: Midday for best visibility
  • Duration: 15-30 minute local flight

Hardware Recommendations

  • Joystick: Logitech Extreme 3D Pro (budget) or Thrustmaster T.16000M (better)
  • Yoke: Honeycomb Alpha + Bravo for full airliner experience
  • Rudder Pedals: Thrustmaster TFRP or MFG Crosswind
  • VR Headset: Meta Quest 3 offers best value/performance ratio
