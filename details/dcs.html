Overview

Digital Combat Simulator (DCS) World is THE definitive combat flight simulator. This is not an arcade game or a simplified sim - it's a study-level platform where aircraft modules take 50-100+ hours to truly master. Every switch, button, and system is modeled with incredible depth, requiring you to learn actual procedures used by real military pilots.

The base game is completely free and includes two aircraft (Su-25T and TF-51D). Additional aircraft modules are sold separately, each representing months or years of development by dedicated teams. From the A-10C Warthog to the F/A-18C Hornet to the F-16C Viper, DCS offers the most authentic modern military flying experience available to civilians.

Perfect for: Military aviation enthusiasts, those who want authentic combat aircraft simulation, pilots willing to invest serious time learning complex systems, and anyone who dreams of flying modern jets with full realism.

Getting Started

For Complete Beginners

Start with the free TF-51D Mustang to learn basic flying. Then try the Su-25T to learn combat systems. Expect to spend 10-20 hours just learning basics. Watch YouTube tutorials from Grim Reapers, Redkite, and others.

First Module to Buy

  • F/A-18C Hornet - Most versatile, carrier ops, air-to-air and ground attack
  • A-10C II Warthog - Best for ground attack, excellent tutorials included
  • F-16C Viper - Modern multirole fighter, very capable
  • F-14B Tomcat - If you loved Top Gun, requires Jester AI or human RIO

Essential Hardware

  • Minimum: Thrustmaster T.16000M HOTAS
  • Better: Thrustmaster Warthog or Virpil/VKB setups
  • Head tracking: TrackIR or smooth-track (phone/webcam)
  • Rudder pedals highly recommended
  • VR headset optional but incredible experience

Learning Resources

  • Chuck's Guides (comprehensive PDFs for each module)
  • YouTube: Grim Reapers, Redkite, Tactical Pascale
