Take On Helicopters

Career Sim

Civilian Helicopter Career Simulation

DeveloperBohemia Interactive
Release DateOctober 2011
PlatformsPC (Steam)
Price$19.99
TypeCivilian Helicopter Sim

Overview

Take On Helicopters is a civilian helicopter simulation game from Bohemia Interactive, the creators of the ARMA series. Unlike military-focused helicopter sims, it puts you in the shoes of a civilian pilot running a helicopter business in Seattle.

The game combines realistic helicopter flight physics with a story-driven career mode where you build your company from the ground up, taking on various civilian contracts from news coverage to firefighting to VIP transport.

Perfect for: Players interested in civilian helicopter operations, career-mode enthusiasts, those wanting a unique helicopter sim experience, and fans of Bohemia's realistic simulation approach.

Key Features

Career Mode

  • Story-driven campaign as helicopter company owner
  • Build your aviation business from scratch
  • Various mission types and contracts
  • Manage finances and operations
  • Hire additional pilots as you grow

Mission Types

  • News and media coverage flights
  • Search and rescue operations
  • VIP and passenger transport
  • Firefighting and emergency response
  • Cargo and sling load operations
  • Sightseeing tours

Locations

  • Detailed Seattle metropolitan area
  • South Asia terrain (DLC)
  • Large open world environments
  • Real-world inspired locations

Helicopters

Take On Helicopters features several civilian helicopter models:

  • Light helicopters for training and tours
  • Medium helicopters for general operations
  • Heavy-lift helicopters for cargo work
  • Each with unique flight characteristics
Flight Model: Built on the ARMA engine, the flight model provides a solid balance between accessibility and realism. It's more forgiving than DCS but more realistic than arcade games.

Why Choose Take On Helicopters?

Unique Focus: One of the few games dedicated entirely to civilian helicopter operations and the business side of aviation.

Career Progression: The story mode and business management add depth beyond just flying missions.

Accessible Flight Model: Realistic enough to be engaging but not so complex as to be overwhelming for newcomers.

Budget Friendly: At $19.99 (often on sale), it's an affordable entry into helicopter simulation.

Modding Support: Bohemia's engine supports community modifications and content.

Considerations

While Take On Helicopters is a unique game, there are some things to keep in mind:

  • Released in 2011 - graphics are dated by modern standards
  • Smaller community compared to DCS or X-Plane
  • Limited helicopter selection compared to add-on markets
  • Some gameplay mechanics feel dated

However, for those interested in civilian helicopter career gameplay, it remains a one-of-a-kind experience that hasn't really been replicated.
