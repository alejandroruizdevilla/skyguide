Arcade Combat

Indie Ace Combat Spiritual Successor

Release DateDecember 2020

VR SupportFull VR Mode

Overview

Project Wingman is an arcade-style combat flight game heavily inspired by the Ace Combat series. Developed by a small indie team, it delivers an experience that rivals AAA titles with its intense aerial combat, dramatic story, and incredible VR implementation.

Set on an alternate Earth ravaged by geological disasters, you play as a mercenary pilot fighting through a gripping 21-mission campaign. The game nails what makes arcade combat flight games fun - tight controls, spectacular battles, and a constant sense of being an unstoppable ace pilot.

Perfect for: Ace Combat fans, arcade combat lovers, VR enthusiasts, players who want action-packed aerial combat without complex systems management.

Key Features

Combat Experience

  • Fast-paced arcade flight mechanics
  • Over 20 aircraft to unlock and fly
  • Satisfying missile and gun combat
  • Boss battles against ace squadrons
  • Epic set-pieces and dramatic missions

Game Modes

  • Full 21-mission campaign with story
  • Conquest mode - roguelike territory control
  • Free Flight for casual flying
  • Multiple difficulty levels

VR Implementation

  • Complete VR support from start to finish
  • Full campaign playable in VR
  • One of the best VR flight experiences available
  • Cockpit interaction and head tracking

Aircraft Roster

Project Wingman features a diverse roster of fictional aircraft inspired by real-world fighters:

  • T/F-4 Chimera - Starter multi-role fighter
  • F/C-16 Viper - Agile air superiority fighter
  • F/E-18 Hornet - Balanced carrier fighter
  • SK.25 Sundowner - Heavy strike aircraft
  • PW-Mk.I - Ultimate unlockable superfighter
  • 20+ total aircraft with unique characteristics
Conquest Mode Tip: Conquest mode adds roguelike elements where you build your mercenary company, hire wingmen, and capture territory. It's essentially an endless mode with high replayability.

Why Choose Project Wingman?

Best Ace Combat Alternative on PC: If you love Ace Combat but game on PC, Project Wingman fills that niche perfectly.

Outstanding VR: The VR mode isn't an afterthought - it's a first-class experience that many consider the best arcade combat VR game available.

Incredible Value: At $24.99, you get a full campaign, conquest mode, and VR support - far less than AAA pricing.

Indie Success Story: Support a small passionate team that delivered a game punching well above its weight class.

Fantastic Soundtrack: The music rivals the legendary Ace Combat soundtracks, with epic orchestral pieces during climactic battles.
