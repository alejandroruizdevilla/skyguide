Overview

Within the Roblox platform, there are numerous flight simulator games created by the community. The most popular is PTFS (Pilot Training Flight Simulator) with 100+ aircraft, but there are many others offering different experiences from airline operations to military combat.

Perfect for kids and beginners, these games provide a friendly introduction to aviation concepts without overwhelming complexity. Play with friends, learn basic controls, and have fun - no expensive hardware or complex setup required.

Perfect for: Kids and young teens, absolute beginners, families wanting to play together, casual multiplayer fun, and introducing aviation concepts in a safe, social environment.

Popular Roblox Flight Games

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Completely free to play
  • Very accessible for kids
  • Social and multiplayer
  • Works on almost any device
  • Great introduction to aviation
  • Play with friends easily
  • No complex setup
  • Safe, moderated environment

Limitations

  • Very simplified flight models
  • Basic graphics (Roblox engine)
  • Not realistic simulation
  • Can have bugs and glitches
  • Some games have aggressive monetization
  • Quality varies greatly

Getting Started

How to Play

  • Create free Roblox account
  • Search for "PTFS" or "flight simulator"
  • Join game and follow tutorial
  • Use keyboard/gamepad/touch controls
  • Play with friends online

Best For

  • Teaching kids about planes
  • Introducing aviation basics
  • Casual multiplayer fun
  • Social gaming with friends
  • First flight sim experience
