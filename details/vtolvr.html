VR Exclusive

Virtual Reality Combat Flight Excellence

PlatformsPC VR (SteamVR, Oculus)

TypeVR Combat Flight Sim

Overview

VTOL VR is a VR-exclusive combat flight game designed specifically for virtual reality. What makes it special is the complete hand-tracked interaction - every button, switch, and throttle in the cockpit is controlled with your actual hands using VR controllers.

Unlike traditional flight sims that use joysticks, VTOL VR lets you physically reach out and flip switches, grab the throttle, and manipulate the stick. This creates an incredibly immersive experience that's both intuitive for beginners and satisfying for veterans.

Perfect for: VR enthusiasts, players who want immersive cockpit interaction, those without dedicated flight hardware, and fans of intuitive flight controls.

Key Features

VR Interaction

  • Full hand-tracked cockpit interaction
  • No HOTAS required - hands are your controllers
  • Intuitive grab-and-manipulate interface
  • Natural throttle and stick movement
  • Touch every button and switch

Aircraft

  • AV-42C VTOL multirole aircraft

Gameplay

  • Full campaign with missions
  • Mission editor for custom scenarios
  • Multiplayer co-op and PvP
  • Workshop mod support
  • Carrier operations

Why Choose VTOL VR?

Best VR Implementation: VTOL VR was built from the ground up for VR, making it one of the most immersive cockpit experiences available.

No Hardware Needed: Your VR controllers are your flight controls. No need to purchase expensive HOTAS setups.

Active Community: Regular updates and a passionate modding community keep the game fresh with new content.

Solo Dev Success: A remarkable achievement from a solo developer that rivals AAA titles in polish and fun factor.
