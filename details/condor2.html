Overview

Condor 2 is THE simulator for glider and sailplane enthusiasts. Used by real glider pilots for training and practicing thermal soaring techniques, it models the art of motorless flight with incredible accuracy. Learn to find and exploit thermals, ridge lift, and wave lift to stay airborne for hours.

This is a specialized simulator focused entirely on soaring - no engines, no weapons, just you, the glider, and the invisible currents of air. It's a zen-like experience that teaches patience, skill, and understanding of meteorology.

Perfect for: Glider pilots and students, anyone interested in soaring, flight simmers wanting something different, meteorology enthusiasts, and those who appreciate the pure art of silent flight.

Getting Started

Learning to Soar

  • Start with training gliders
  • Learn to find thermals (rising air columns)
  • Practice circling to stay in lift
  • Master ridge soaring basics
  • Join online community for tips

First Flights

  • Use winch or aerotow launch
  • Find thermal markers
  • Circle in lift to gain altitude
  • Glide to next thermal
  • Learn to read the sky and terrain

Progression

  • Complete training tasks
  • Try online competitions
  • Learn cross-country soaring
  • Master wave lift for high altitude
  • Compete in multiplayer events
