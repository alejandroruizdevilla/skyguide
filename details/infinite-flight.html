Overview

Infinite Flight is THE premier mobile flight simulator, offering an incredibly realistic aviation experience on iOS and Android devices. With global HD scenery, live human ATC, multiplayer with 1000+ pilots simultaneously, and 60+ detailed aircraft, it rivals desktop simulators in features while fitting in your pocket.

What sets Infinite Flight apart is its vibrant community. Live ATC is staffed by real human controllers, virtual airlines run scheduled operations, and regular fly-in events attract hundreds of pilots. It's a complete aviation ecosystem on mobile.

Perfect for: Mobile device owners who want serious flight simulation, airline pilots practicing procedures on the go, anyone wanting live human ATC, and casual to serious simmers who fly from anywhere.

Pros & Cons

Strengths

  • Best mobile flight simulator available
  • Live human ATC (IFATC)
  • Massive multiplayer community
  • Global HD scenery
  • Regular events and fly-ins
  • Virtual airlines for structure
  • Works on phones and tablets
  • Active development

Limitations

  • Requires Pro subscription for full features
  • Mobile controls less precise than desktop
  • Aircraft systems simplified vs desktop sims
  • Graphics not as detailed as MSFS
  • Requires good internet for multiplayer

Getting Started

Free vs Pro

  • Free: Limited aircraft, solo flight only
  • Pro ($9.99/mo): All aircraft, live multiplayer, global scenery
  • Pro required for serious use

Tips

  • Start with GA aircraft to learn
  • Study IFATC rules before using live ATC
  • Join virtual airline for community
  • Use autopilot for long flights
  • Tablet recommended over phone
